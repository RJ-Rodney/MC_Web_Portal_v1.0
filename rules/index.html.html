General Server Goals:

At the heart of Project: WARJORN, our mission is to cultivate a thriving, immersive, and inclusive community where players can engage in rich roleplaying experiences, collaborative worldbuilding, and meaningful social interactions. We strive to create a dynamic, player-driven ecosystem where every participant has the freedom to shape their own story while contributing to a larger, evolving narrative.

Key Objectives:

  • A Safe & Welcoming Environment
  • Foster a respectful and harassment-free space where players of all backgrounds feel valued and empowered.
  • Implement clear, fair moderation to ensure that conflicts are resolved constructively and that the server remains a positive place for creativity and interaction.

  • Deep, Engaging Roleplay
  • Encourage character-driven storytelling, where players can develop complex personas, form alliances, engage in political intrigue, or wage epic battles—all within a living, breathing world.
  • Support diverse RP styles, from casual social interactions to high-stakes narrative arcs, ensuring there’s something for every kind of roleplayer.

  • Player-Led Economy & Worldbuilding
  • Establish a fully player-run economy, where trade, crafting, and resource management are driven by community decisions and market dynamics.
  • Allow players to shape the world through settlements, factions, and historical events, ensuring that the server’s lore grows organically from player actions and collaborations.

  • Freedom of Playstyle
  • Provide opportunities for both casual and dedicated players to thrive, whether they prefer diplomacy, warfare, commerce, or exploration.
  • Avoid restrictive mechanics, instead promoting emergent gameplay where creativity and player agency lead to unexpected and exciting outcomes.

  • Community & Collaboration
  • Encourage teamwork through guilds, factions, and shared goals, while also allowing for rivalries and conflicts (within respectful boundaries).
  • Host regular events, contests, and lore-building sessions to keep the community engaged and invested in the world’s evolution.

By upholding these principles, Project: WARJORN aims to be more than just a server—it will be a persistent, player-crafted universe where every member can leave their mark.

Rule Description

Notes

1.1

Avoid the use of swear words in public chats, as to create a safe environment for everyone.

1.2

DO NOT use slurs directed towards protected classes in accordance to the Civil Rights Act of 1964 and future laws created in the United States.

Clarifications will be made and warnings will be handed out.

1.3

DO NOT use sexually charged language in public chats, as to create a safe environment for everyone.

1.4

Hate speech and other generally targeted harassment is NOT allowed, including but not limited to: the use of information (public or private) to induce a reaction or get something out of someone, the use of vulgar language to try to make someone leave an area or the server entirely, jokes based on protected classes defined above, etc…

1.5

No self promotion / advertising in public or private chats. If found, punishments will not be taken as a chat offense, rather a grave offense and lead to possibly permanent termination.

1.6

Spams and scams are not allowed, this includes spam in terms of spamming chats, and spam in terms of unwanted material. Scamming is disallowed in public chats in terms of phishing and other malicious attacks.

Scamming in game is generally allowed, as long as it is not malicious scamming outside of Minecraft.

1.7

No backseat moderation, this includes telling people to stop breaking rules in a childish way, and begging staff to deal with a situation. The best course of action is to (indirectly) point out the rules and bring the issue up in a ticket.

Do not argue in public chat—take issues to staff via tickets/DMs.

1.8

Arguing with staff over punishments will result in further action. Appeal bans properly.
